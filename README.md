Swift Bite - SDG 3

Project Summary 

Many individuals struggle to consistently track their daily nutritional intake despite understanding its importance for health. Simple habits such as monitoring calorie intake, balancing macronutrients, and staying adequately hydrated are often neglected due to the lack of a personalised and easy-to-use tool. Existing solutions such as generic diet plans and handwritten food diaries fail to account for individual differences in body type, lifestyle, and personal goals. Poor dietary habits over time can contribute to serious health conditions including obesity, malnutrition, diabetes, and heart disease. To address this, SwiftBite is a mobile application designed to help users manage their daily nutrition in a simple, personalised, and motivating way — making healthy eating more achievable in everyday life.
